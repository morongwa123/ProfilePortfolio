Bardeli Booking Management System

A full-stack booking platform built for a local catering business, replacing manual phone and paper-based reservations.

The problem

Bardeli managed bookings entirely by phone and paper — no central record of reservations, deposits, payments or client communication. That made it slow for customers to book and hard for the business to keep track of what was actually happening.

My role

I led front-end development and UX design as part of a three-person team, working through the full development lifecycle — requirements gathering, design, build, testing and handover.

What we built

A web-based system letting customers make and manage their own reservations online, while giving the business full visibility over bookings, deposits, payments and client communication in one place.

Outcome

Delivered a fully functional product from concept to handover — replacing the business's manual process with a system they could actually run bookings through.
