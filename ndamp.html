Nexus Digital Agency Platform

A self-built SaaS-style dashboard for a fictional digital marketing agency, designed to demonstrate business analysis, dashboard design and front-end development together.

The concept

NDAMP is built for a fictional agency managing campaigns, websites, branding and digital advertising across multiple clients. It's a self-directed project — not a client build — designed specifically to demonstrate how I'd approach real agency software: client management, campaign tracking, task workflows and business intelligence in one system.

Why I built it

Most student portfolio projects are simple CRUD apps. I wanted something that looked and behaved like real business software — with role-based access, decision-support analytics and the kind of dashboard a manager would actually use to make calls.

What it includes

Role-based access for Admins, Managers, Employees and Clients. A dashboard with KPI cards and charts covering revenue, campaign status and team workload. Full client and campaign management modules, task tracking, and an analytics section styled as an executive business intelligence view — including automatically generated business insights.

Tech stack

React, Chart.js / Recharts for data visualisation, React Context API for state management. Built in phases — starting with a complete UI on mock data, then layering in CRUD functionality, authentication and a real backend.
